Privacy Policy

At Aero Aviation, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you interact with our website and services.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

  • Request a quote or make a booking.
  • Contact us via email or phone.
  • Subscribe to our newsletters or marketing communications.
  • Provide feedback or participate in surveys.

This information may include your name, email address, phone number, travel preferences, and any other details necessary to fulfill your requests.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including:

  • To provide and manage our charter services.
  • To communicate with you regarding your inquiries, bookings, and service updates.
  • To improve our website and services.
  • To send you promotional materials or information about new services, if you have opted in to receive them.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties without your consent, except as described below:

  • Operating Carriers: We share necessary information with the third-party direct air carriers who operate your flights to facilitate your charter service.
  • Service Providers: We may share information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our website, conducting our business, or serving our clients, provided they agree to keep this information confidential.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information when we believe release is appropriate to comply with the law, enforce our site policies, or protect ours or others' rights, property, or safety.

Data Security

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information when you place a request or enter, submit, or access your personal information.

Your Consent

By using our site, you consent to our privacy policy.

Changes to Our Privacy Policy

If we decide to change our Privacy Policy, we will post those changes on this page.

Contacting Us

If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy, you may contact us using the information on our Contact Us page.
